Our Sunset!

John Lindsey from The Great Highway Gallery is organzing a community photo collaboration!

Everyone has pics of the Sunset!

Rather than being a cliche, let's focus on that ubiquity! We're going to create a large scale installation collage assembled from YOUR pictures of the Sunset!

We're looking for hi-res pics to use - PNG, jpeg, or tiff, minimum 2 MB, Max 25 MB.

Send them to oursunset@sfstokefest.com!
